About Me


Software engineering is hard. Running 100 miles is harder.

When I was student at CU Boulder I was hit by a car riding my bike to a summer linear algebra course. I was lucky I was not seriously injured, however I wasn't able to run more then 4 miles without severe pain. Fast forward to August 2016. After 3 attempts and countless hours of traiing, I crossed the finishline of the Leadville 100 ultra marathon. Anything is possible with focus and hard work.

I enjoy doing hard things, whether that is running in the mountains .
